Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our API pricing and plans.

What is the difference between Demo and paid plans?
The Demo plan is free and designed for testing and prototyping. It provides limited API calls (10K/month) and access to basic endpoints. Paid plans unlock higher rate limits, full historical data, on-chain/DEX data, NFT data, and dedicated support — everything you need for production applications.
How do I get an API Key?
Sign up for a CoinGecko account and navigate to the Developer Dashboard. You can generate your API key instantly — no credit card required for the Demo plan. For paid plans, simply choose your tier and complete the checkout process.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. We'll prorate the difference on upgrades.
What happens if I exceed my API call limit?
When you reach your monthly call limit, subsequent requests will return a 429 (Too Many Requests) response. We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage via email. You can upgrade your plan anytime to increase your limits without interruption.
Does CoinGecko offer WebSocket support?
Yes! WebSocket real-time streaming is available on the Pro plan. It provides live price updates, trade data, and order book changes with minimal latency — ideal for trading platforms and real-time dashboards.
How often is the data refreshed?
Price and market data is refreshed every 30–60 seconds across all plans. On-chain data and NFT floor prices update on a slightly longer interval depending on network conditions. WebSocket connections (Pro plan) deliver data in near real-time.
